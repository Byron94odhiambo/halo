Our Goals

HALO KENYA believes that  Healthy people are the foundation for healthy economies.To bridge this gap and ensure equitable healthcare provision, addressing disparities is critical, HALO KENYA is making strides in this.

Education

Education helps to reduce inequalities and to reach gender equality. It also empowers people everywhere to live more healthy and sustainable lives. Education is also crucial to fostering tolerance between people and contributes to more peaceful societies.

Climate Change

HALO KENYA believes that If left unchecked, climate change will cause average global temperatures to increase beyond 3°C, and will adversely affect every ecosystem. Already, we are seeing how climate change can exacerbate storms and disasters, and threats such as food and water scarcity, which can lead to conflict. Doing nothing will end up costing us a lot more than if we take action now. We make commitments to make a difference.

Gender

Women and girls represent half of the world’s population and therefore also half of its potential. But gender inequality persists everywhere and stagnates social progress. Advancing gender equality is critical to all areas of a healthy society, from reducing poverty to promoting the health, education, protection and the well-being of girls and boys. HALO KENYA is gender sensitive and makes commitment to make trans formative strides in norms change

Our Core Values

Accountability

We believe that being accountable is not just a commitment but a responsibility we owe to our beneficiaries, partners, donors, and the communities we serve.

Intergrity

We are committed to upholding the trust placed in us by our beneficiaries, donors, partners, and the communities we serve.

Commitment to service

Service is more than a duty; it is a calling that drives us to make a meaningful difference in the lives of the communities we serve.

Equality

We believe that every individual, regardless of their background, gender, race, ethnicity, religion, or socio-economic status, deserves equal opportunities, rights, and respect.
